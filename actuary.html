Actuary

Average Salary: $108,000 per year (Source: BLS)

Day-to-Day: Actuaries analyze statistical data to estimate risk in insurance, finance, and other industries. Their daily tasks involve complex mathematical modeling and interpreting probabilities.

Education Required: Typically a Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics, Statistics, or Actuarial Science, followed by professional exams.
