Accountant

Average Salary: $72,000 per year (Source: BLS)

Day-to-Day: Accountants manage financial records, prepare tax returns, and help organizations manage budgets and financial planning. Their work involves detail-oriented analysis and reporting.

Education Required: Typically a Bachelor's degree in Accounting or Finance, along with certifications like CPA for advancement.
