Lawyer

Average Salary: $125,000 per year (Source: BLS)

Day-to-Day: Lawyers research legal issues, prepare documents, and represent clients in court and negotiations. Their daily tasks can vary significantly depending on their practice area.

Education Required: A Bachelor's degree followed by a Juris Doctor (JD) from a law school and passing the bar exam.
