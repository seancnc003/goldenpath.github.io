Electrician

Average Salary: $56,000 per year (Source: BLS)

Day-to-Day: Electricians install, maintain, and repair electrical systems in residential, commercial, or industrial settings. Their work is hands-on and requires troubleshooting complex wiring systems.

Education Required: Typically a combination of technical training, apprenticeship, and sometimes vocational schooling.
