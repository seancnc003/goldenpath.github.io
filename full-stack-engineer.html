Full-Stack Engineer

Average Salary: $95,000 per year (Source: BLS)

Day-to-Day: Full-Stack Engineers design, develop, and maintain both client- and server-side software applications. They work with databases, APIs, and front-end frameworks, often collaborating on project teams.

Education Required: Typically a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field.
